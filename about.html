The Story

Forged by Fire, Built on Flavor

Ken has been obsessed with cooking since he was old enough to stand at a grill. What started as backyard experiments with a charcoal kettle turned into a full-blown passion for smoke, fire, and the craft of making food that genuinely surprises people.

Ken's Flavor Forge started as a way to share that passion — and Twitch turned out to be the perfect place for it. Cooking live means nothing is edited, nothing is faked, and the chat keeps it honest. When something goes wrong on stream, you'll hear about it. When something goes right, you'll know exactly how to replicate it.

Every recipe on this site was cooked live first. No food styling, no multiple takes. Real heat, real results, real flavor.
