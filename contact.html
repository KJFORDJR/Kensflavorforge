Quick Answers

Common Questions

When does Ken stream?

Every Sunday at Noon Eastern. Follow on Twitch for notifications.

Can I request a recipe?

Yes — drop it in Twitch chat during a stream or use the contact form above. The Flavor Crew has gotten plenty of dishes onto the menu.

Are the recipes free?

Every single one. No paywalls, no subscriptions. Good food shouldn't be locked behind a sign-up form.

How do I reach Ken about a collab?

Use the contact form above or reach out directly by email. Include details about your brand/channel and what you have in mind.

Where are past streams saved?

VODs are available on Twitch for a limited time, and highlights go up on YouTube. Check the Stream page for links.

Does Ken do in-person events?

Occasionally — follow on social media for announcements. Local events and pop-ups get posted there first.
